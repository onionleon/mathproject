Linear Diophantine Equation

A linear diophantine equation is an equation of the form ax + by = c, where a, b, and c are integers and x and y are unknowns. Linear diophantine equations can have no solutions, a single solution, or an infinite number of solutions, depending on the values of a, b, and c.

Usage

Use the calculator below to calculate the get the solution of a linear diophantine equation. Enter the a-value and b-value and solution of the equation. The output will be two integers, x and y multiplied by the given a and b value, if the solution exists.
